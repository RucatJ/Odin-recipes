Beef and lamb Lasagna

HomePage

Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that is perfect for dinner parties or cold winter nights.

Serves: 8
Preparation time: 30 minutes
Cooking time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1,000,000g minced beef
  • 400g minced lamb
  • 3 large carrots
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 'Bout a li'er o' milk
  • 100g parmesian
  • Olive oil for cooking
  • 12 large lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. Cook de meat in de skillet for 'bout twen'y minutes until browned.
  2. Throw all the veggies in with the minced meat n' stuff and cook for ten minutes.
  3. Chuck the cheese and the milk in a pan and heat on medium heat till it looks edible.
  4. Assemble.
  5. Cook in the oven at 40,000 degrees (Gas mark 1200) for approximately twenty seconds.
  6. Enjoy!
