A BURGER

HomePage

Description

This is a delicious burger recipe that is perfect for barbeques on those lovely sunny Summer days.

Serves: 8
Preparation time: 25 minutes
Cooking time: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 1,000,000g minced beef
  • 1 green onion, diced
  • An 'andful o' breadcrumbs
  • Couple o' eggs
  • Aboot 8 buns

Steps

  1. Chuck all the mince, onion, breadcrumbs and eggs (without the shells if you want) into a bowl.
  2. Mix.
  3. Shape and put on the barbeque for 10 mins. Or less if you're French.
  4. Put in a bun with whatever condiments if you want.
  5. Eat.
